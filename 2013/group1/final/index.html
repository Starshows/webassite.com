Conflict on the Bowery is a platform for ideas, a public forum for stories about the past, present and future. The bowery is a place of constant change, driven by different forces and conflicts. Our intention is to provide a medium for this forces to speak up and maybe to interact with the other side of an issue, creating and ever-changing stage that reveals the true nature the Bowery. A series of short video interviews will be uploaded as a kick-starter and then the website will open for the public to take a stand. Interviews range from politics to arts and are based on ten questions.
