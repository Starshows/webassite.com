Conflicts on Bowery

submission about
ask a question to people in bowery
  • What is the major conflict of the Bowery today?
  • Do you think there is a certain type of a community that exists in Bowery?
  • Who are the influential groups on the Bowery?
  • How has the presence of the New Museum influenced the Bowery?
  • Do you think this part of the city has an identity? How is it different from other parts?
  • How has the Bowery changed in the last 5 years?
  • How do you feel about zoning changes that allow high-rise developments to be built along the Bowery?
  • What would you change about the Bowery?
  • What would you like to preserve about the Bowery?

Conflict on the Bowery is a platform for ideas, a public forum for stories about the past, present and future. The bowery is a place of constant change, driven by different forces and conflicts. Our intention is to provide a medium for this forces to speak up and maybe to interact with the other side of an issue, creating and ever-changing stage that reveals the true nature the Bowery. A series of short video interviews will be uploaded as a kick-starter and then the website will open for the public to take a stand. Interviews range from politics to arts and are based on ten questions.
